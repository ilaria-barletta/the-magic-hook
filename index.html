About me


Welcome everyone!

This is a magical place, really. One that I would have loved to find when, during the first lockdown of my life, I decided I wanted to become a crochet master.

My name is Ilaria, I have always loved crafts and creativity has been with me my whole life. If you have a passion for wool and hooks or if you are simply considering to lear a new skill and crochet interests you, this is the perfect place for you.

Here we'll go through the basics, you'll understand quickly what you need to start and how to decide what materials to use for your work.

I'll give you some templates to follow as well, simple instructions that will take you from zero to hero in no time.

Follow me and get in touch, maybe we'll meet at the next workshop.

Hooks at the ready: let's do some magic!
